Gnib


Takes bing search results and displays them in reverse order. The lowest priority result is now the highest





About Gnib


Sick of getting the most common websites as top results? Want to see the unknown parts of the internet (hopefully not)? Well Ginb is here for you! This search takes all the bing results and reverses the order. All those websites that would never be seen by anyone, are now the top picks for whatever search you want.
